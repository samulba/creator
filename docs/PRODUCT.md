# Creator — Product Specification

## Product Vision

Creator is a professional AI-powered video production platform that transforms raw Dead by Daylight gameplay recordings into high-quality long-form YouTube videos with minimal manual work.

The goal is not to generate generic AI content.

The goal is to create entertaining, story-driven YouTube videos based on real gameplay events.

## Primary User Flow

1. User uploads raw Dead by Daylight gameplay.
2. Creator processes and analyzes the gameplay.
3. Important events, chases, funny moments, mistakes, and turning points are detected.
4. The system identifies the strongest story for the video.
5. A structured narration script is created.
6. AI voiceover is generated.
7. An automated edit plan is created.
8. The gameplay, voiceover, game audio, cuts, captions, zooms, and effects are combined.
9. The finished YouTube video is rendered.
10. The user reviews and downloads the final result.

## Initial Product Goal

The first version must focus on one thing:

Producing high-quality long-form Dead by Daylight YouTube videos.

Target video length:

Approximately 8–15 minutes, depending on the source material and story.

## Core Principles

- Quality is more important than maximum automation.
- The final video must feel intentionally edited.
- Gameplay events must be understood before narration is written.
- Strong gameplay moments should not be ruined by unnecessary cuts or narration.
- Every video should have a clear story or narrative angle.
- AI output must be reviewable and reproducible.
- The system should automate repetitive production work while preserving creative quality.

## Out of Scope for the Initial Version

Do not build these features unless explicitly added to the roadmap:

- YouTube Shorts
- TikTok videos
- Instagram Reels
- automatic social media posting
- public SaaS billing
- subscriptions
- teams or organizations
- mobile applications
- unrelated game support
- public marketplace features

## Success Criteria

Creator is successful when a user can upload raw gameplay and receive a high-quality long-form YouTube video that requires little or no manual editing before publishing.
